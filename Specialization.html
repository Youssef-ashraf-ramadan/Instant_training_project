about Faculty

The Faculty of Commerce and Business Administration (as one of the faculties of Helwan University) was established by Prime Minister Decision No. 924 in 1975. The faculty aims to prepare specialists in management, accounting and foreign trade that are compatible with the needs of the labor market.
